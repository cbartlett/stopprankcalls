Stop Prank Calls

Annoyed by prank phone calls?

There are numerous free and commercial services across the web that allow people to send prank recorded phone calls anonymously from fake numbers.

The calls stop here

All the most popular prank calling services are listed below with links to opt out or block your number. Find the service pranking you or block them all for good measure.
